Penny Stock Education

Understanding Penny Stocks

Penny stocks are typically shares of companies with market capitalizations below $1 billion, often trading at low prices with limited liquidity and analyst coverage.

Key Features We Analyze:

Valuation Metrics
  • P/E Ratio: Price-to-earnings ratio indicates if stock is over/undervalued
  • P/B Ratio: Price-to-book ratio shows market premium to book value
  • PEG Ratio: P/E divided by growth rate for growth-adjusted valuation
Risk Factors
  • Low Liquidity: Difficulty buying/selling large quantities
  • High Volatility: Significant price swings in short periods
  • Limited Information: Sparse analyst coverage and financial data
